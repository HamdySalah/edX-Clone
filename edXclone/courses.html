More about partnering

edX regularly partners with many different types of organizations from all around the world - academic institutions (from large research universities to polytechnics and liberal arts colleges), non-profit institutions, national governments, non-governmental organizations (NGOs), and multinational corporations in connection with its MicroMasters™ program and other educational programs and related services.


Where there is strong mission alignment with edX and the ability to commit strategic, course content, and/or financial contributions back to the consortium, we are eager to hear from your institution on where it is today and where it is planning to go with online/blended learning and MOOCs. In turn, for participating member institutions in the MicroMasters program and other educational programs, edX offers its members a variety of technical, marketing, and educational services (i.e., training, onboarding, high-level program management, learner technical support, course strategy, design, build, run and re-run consultations involving data analytics, and so on).

If you are interested in partnering with edX to offer courses and programs, please visit partnerships.edx.org.

If you are a business or corporation interested in using edX to train and develop your employees, please visit business.edx.org.
